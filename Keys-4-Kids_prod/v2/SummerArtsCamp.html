Creative Arts Camp

About

Come explore and discover your unique talents through music and creative performing arts! The Keys for Kids Music and Creative Arts Camp has something for everyone who loves music in a fun, engaging, musical journey. This camp offers group piano, singing, guitar, percussions, musical theater, costume and set design and much more! Students are encouraged to adventurously explore their individuality while learning what it means to collaborate as an ensemble. Musicians also enjoy (weather permitting) daily outdoor activities.

Campers 6-12yo

Fill your child's school vacationn / summer with the welcome sounds of music and laughter. The Creative Arts Camp is an introduction to all things music and performing arts. No previous music instruction required.

CIT Program for 14-16yo

Our CIT (Counselor in Training) program is designed for the older music student age 14-16, merging their participation in all music activities and classes with inspiring illustration of their passion for music. In the process, CIT musicians are introduced to new music instruments and techniques while gaining important lifelong teamwork and leadership skills. At the end of the session CIT participants receive a “Counselor in Training” certificate.

Instructors

All professional staff members are experienced instructors in their specialty area — music education, drama, art, theater, childhood education and development. Staff members are chosen for their outstanding skills and their dedication to working with children.

Refund Policy

In order to maintain the small “chamber" size at the camp and allow for individual attention and progress, the refund policy is as follows:

~ A full refund, minus $50 registration fee is granted with a written notice of withdrawal from the Summer Camp program received and confirmed by May 1st.
~ After May 1st: a full refund, minus $150 fee, is granted, provided there is a written notice of withdrawal from the Summer Camp program received and confirmed at least 6 full weeks before the first day of the session.
~ Withdrawals with less than 6 full weeks notice before the 1st day of the registered camp session are ineligble for a refund.
